A conversation with Martin Dougiamas about ICT and Education

Posted at — Feb 17, 2020

Martin Dougiamas is the creator of the Moodle open-source software project. I have collaborated with him extensively in the past. A few weeks ago, Martin was kind enough to come to visit me at LISA (Laboratori d’Inovació i Suport Audiovisual del ICE de la UPC) where we had a conversation for over an hour and a half about Education, Innovation, and many other things.

Martin is going to edit and trim the audio for his podcast, the OpenEdTec Podcast. The conversation is in English, but the YouTube video has decent subtitles. It does a better job with Martin’s English than with my Maresme English. I hope you enjoy it.
